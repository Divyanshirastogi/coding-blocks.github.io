ADMISSIONS OPEN FOR AUGUST BATCHES
After a succesful summer season, Coding Blocks is now ready to start a new set of batches in August. These courses will be taught by mentors from IIT, IIIT, DTU, PEC and other renowned colleges who have worked with companies like Facebook, Sony, Micromax, SanDisk, Nagarro, etc. Sign up now to join Coding Blocks and take a giant leap towards a hugely successful career.

WEBINARS ORGANIZED
KOTLIN WEBINAR
Coding Blocks organized a Live Webinar on our YouTube channel on July 12 to help our followers with the implementation of Kotlin in Android. Google announced support for Kotlin during its I/O event earlier this year, and we at Coding Blocks decided to stay updated with the times. The webinar was conducted by Arnav Gupta and was very well received by budding Android developers.
Watch Video
BITMASKING WEBINAR
Coding Blocks organized a Live Webinar on our  YouTube channel  on July 3 to help our followers with Bitmasking and Interview problems. Bitwise operators are the most efficient operators when it comes to speed, and many new concepts and techniques were discussed in the webinar. The webinar was conducted by Prateek Narang and was very well received by all attendees.

CODING BLOCKS GETS NEW TALENT
CAST 2.0
Coding Blocks conducted CAST 2.0 for admission to our foundation courses. The aptitude based test was held on July 23 and attempted by over 600 students. Through this test, Coding Blocks has awarded scholarships of up to 75% off the course fees of foundation courses.
IIIT DELHI HIRING
Recently, Coding Blocks visited the campus of IIIT Delhi on a hiring drive, looking for brilliant coders with the passion to teach. Applicants had to go through a rigorous system of written tests, interviews as well as demo classes. We are proud to have our new team members on board starting June 2018.
BOOTCAMPS ORGANIZED
COMPETITIVE CODING
Coding Blocks conducted another succesful bootcamp on Competitive Programming last month. It was conducted by Prateek Narang and other mentors. The bootcamp helped budding coders grasp important concepts (Number Theory, Segment Trees, Lazy Propagation, Bitmasking, Dynamic Programming and Game Theory) while solving many sample problems. It was a successful Bootcamp and students learned a lot.
INTERVIEW PREPARATION
Coding Blocks organized a Bootcamp on Interview Preparation to give third and fourth year students a Midas touch before their final placements. It was conducted by Arnav Gupta and Rajesh Sachdev where we covered non-tech topics such as Operating System, DBMS, System Design, etc. asked in various company interviews. Students even got a mock skype interview to get a real world feel of the procedure.
