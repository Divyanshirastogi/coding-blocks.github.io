FOURTH PHYSICAL CENTRE INAUGURATED
Coding Blocks takes great pride in announcing that our fourth physical centre is now open in Greater Noida. Located in the heart of Greater Noida at a walking distance from Pari Chowk, this centre is aimed to cater to the requests of the many students who earlier had to travel over sixty kilometres to attend classes. BOOTCAMPS AND WORKSHOPS ORGANIZED
COMPETITIVE CODING
Coding Blocks conducted a week-long bootcamp on Competitive Programming at the Dwarka Centre. It was conducted by Prateek Narang, Mayank Ladia, Vishal Panwar and Saransh Gupta and attended by over sixty people. The bootcamp covered all important concepts, their applications, and a lot of sample problems. Topics such as Number Theory, Segment Trees, Lazy Propagation, Bitmasking, Dynamic Programming and Game Theory were covered in depth.
MACHINE LEARNING
Coding Blocks organized a Bootcamp on Machine learning in the Pitampura centre by our main ML mentor, Shubham Dokania. It was attended by over fifty people and covered the important aspects and concepts of Machine Learning in a concise form. Topics ranging from Python Introduction, Linear Algebra, Statistics, Numpy and Decision trees to Features and Dimensions, Regression, Neural Networks, Vector Machines and Transfer Learning were covered.
PYTHON BASICS
Coding Blocks organized a bootcamp on the basics of Python at the Greater Noida centre. It covered Python from Introduction, Variables, Functions and Conditional Operations to Dicts, Classes, Objects, Lambdas and Maps. Attended by over fifty students, it was conducted by Nikhil Kumar Singh, a DTU student and an ardent Python developer. Known as IndianPythonista, his blog and YouTube channel are among the go to resources for Python developers.
INTERVIEW QUESTIONS
A workshop on technical interview questions was conducted by Prateek bhaiyya at DTU, and was attended by over 200 students. Attendees were guided about placement procedures of various companies, and question asking styles were discussed. Important topics such as Recursion, Backtracking, Trees and Dynamic Programming were covered too. On a rainy day in the middle of vacations, the enthusiasm of students to learn was really heartening.
